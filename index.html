History of Qadosh Threads

QADOSH THREADS is a thrift store founded from a shared passion for clothing and thrift fashion. The owners, Francis Paglicawan and John Felix, were inspired after observing the growing trend of thrift businesses online, where old and branded clothes are being successfully resold.
